## 1. Problem Statement

Wekeza Bank currently faces significant challenges in managing customer relationships effectively, resulting in:

### 1.1 Customer Experience Issues
- **Fragmented Data:** Customer information scattered across 5+ disconnected systems
- **Slow Service:** Average case resolution time of 72 hours (industry benchmark: 24 hours)
- **Inconsistent Experience:** Different service quality across channels (branch, call center, digital)
- **Limited Personalization:** Inability to provide tailored service based on customer history
- **Poor Communication:** Manual, delayed responses to customer inquiries

### 1.2 Operational Inefficiencies
- **Manual Processes:** 60% of customer service tasks are manual and paper-based
- **Data Duplication:** Repetitive data entry across systems leading to errors
- **Lack of Visibility:** No real-time view of customer interactions and cases
- **Resource Waste:** Significant time spent on administrative tasks vs. customer service
- **Limited Insights:** Inability to derive actionable insights from customer data

### 1.3 Business Impact
- **Low Customer Satisfaction:** CSAT score of 65% (below industry average of 80%)
- **High Churn Rate:** 15% annual customer churn (industry average: 10%)
- **Missed Revenue:** Low cross-sell ratio of 1.2 products per customer (potential: 2.5)
- **Compliance Risks:** Incomplete audit trails and data quality issues
- **Competitive Disadvantage:** Falling behind digitally-advanced competitors

**Quantified Problem:**
- Lost revenue: $2M annually due to churn and missed opportunities
- Operational costs: $500K annually in manual processing
- Customer dissatisfaction: 35% of customers rate service as "poor" or "fair"

## 2. Proposed Solution

### 2.1 Solution Overview
Implement **Wekeza CRM** - a comprehensive, bank-grade Customer Relationship Management system that centralizes customer data, automates workflows, and provides AI-powered insights to deliver exceptional customer experience.

### 2.2 Key Features

#### Core CRM Capabilities
- **360° Customer View:** Unified view of all customer data, interactions, and transactions
- **Intelligent Case Management:** Automated routing, SLA tracking, and resolution workflows
- **Omni-Channel Engagement:** Seamless experience across branch, digital, and mobile channels
- **Campaign Management:** Targeted marketing campaigns with performance tracking

#### AI & Automation (Phase 2)
- **Next Best Actions:** AI-powered recommendations for customer engagement
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Analyze customer sentiment from all interactions
- **Workflow Automation:** Automate repetitive tasks and approvals
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Instant alerts for users and customers
- **Advanced Analytics:** Comprehensive dashboards with predictive insights

#### Communication & Reporting (Phase 3)
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Business messaging with delivery tracking
- **USSD Banking:** Mobile money services for unbanked/underbanked
- **Advanced Reporting:** Scheduled reports with multi-format exports
- **Self-Service Portal:** Customer portal for account management

### 2.3 Technology Approach
- **Modern Stack:** .NET 8, SQL Server, cloud-native architecture
- **Clean Architecture:** Maintainable, testable, scalable codebase
- **API-First Design:** RESTful APIs for seamless integration
- **Security by Design:** Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- **Mobile-Ready:** Responsive design for all devices
